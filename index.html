Karnaugh Map (K-Map) Simplifier — Unique Feature

The K-Map Simplifier is DIGISIM's unique feature that automatically minimizes Boolean expressions. After building your circuit, click K-MAP in the toolbar. Enter your truth table output values and the engine will group adjacent 1s to find the Simplified Sum of Products (SOP) expression. This helps reduce gate count and optimize your circuit.

Supports 2-variable (2×2), 3-variable (2×4), and 4-variable (4×4) K-Maps with visual grouping and Boolean expression output.
